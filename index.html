Automated Robot Bed Bathing close

The central idea of this project was "How can we use robotics to automate nursing tasks?" The idea of automating bathing of elderly and disabled patients was suggested by mentor. Basically I could draw a stain on the mannequin, which the Kinect would detect. I would then compute a cleaning plan for the robot to execute. I programmed the Kinect in C++, manipulating its pointclouds with PCL and OpenCV. The robot was programmed in Java.
